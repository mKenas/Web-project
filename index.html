Closing the digital gap, one connection at a time.

As technology is more involved in our daily life, there is one group that seems to fall behind the rest, the older generation. They didn’t grow up with smart phones, advanced computers and may find it challenging to adapt. Our vision is to close the increasing digital gap between the old and the young. This is important as many aspects of our everyday life is rapidly being digitized* . The post office, bank are all moving towards and adhering to the digital craze. Wanna check your bank balance? You’ve got to have knowledge on how to log in through a complicated system and navigate through the website to do so.

How it works

By closing this gap, not only do we hope to bring people closer, but also to make everyday life easier for our users. Be my IT guide will be at your service when your loved ones are not there to help you. The user will be able to talk directly to the IT guide, making it easier and faster to solve the problem. Registration/logging into the app will be quick and easy, as the user may use facebook, Face/Touch ID and email

Features

Anyone can use it!

Registration process is easier than ever! By entering your device PIN code or even your Face/Touch ID we take care of making new user for you so that you would be one click away from getting the help.

Get help in your language!

The app will allow users to select the languages they speak then will connect people who need help with guides who understand their language in order to ensure that the help is on it best level.

Chat/video

Having both chat and video sharing allows the user to choose how they want to receive the IT support, thus making the app user-oriented.

Evaluation system

Our evaluation system allows users to rate the help provided by the IT guide and let the guides leave a feedback about thier experience. This rating will help us to improve the service by connecting people with the guides based on their rating score.

Seamless screen sharing = Easy and concrete help!

To aid the user in the most optimal way, we offer a screen sharing alternative, where the users can share their screen with the IT-guide to get help in a quick and easy way.

It's not just awesome! It's secure!

Securing the privacy of users is our top priority. The screen sharing would not give access to user device, however thanks to our smart video recording feature, it will send a video record of the user screen and restricts the guides’ access to the users personal information. Every user-to-guide interactions will be recorded, to ensure that there may not be security breach.
