Closing the digital gap, one connection at a time.

The world as a whole is becoming more tech oriented, however there is one group that seems to be left behind, the older generation. Our vision is to close this digital gap, so that everyone may reap the benefits of the technological development. Be my IT-Guide introduces video calls as an alternative way to get help, therefore helping the older generation getting accustomed to the digital age.

How it works

Be my IT-guide is dedicated in helping to solve day-to-day tech problems. We connect the helpers to those that need their service. Users can talk directly to their guides through screen-sharing and chat/video calls making IT problem solving quick and easy.

Features

Registration in a flash!

Registration process is easier than ever! By entering your device PIN code or even by using your Face/Touch ID we take care of making new user for you so that you would be one click away from getting the help.

Get help in your language!

The app lets users select their choice of language to ensure that language barriers may not be a factor in the problem solving.

Chat/video

Having both chat and video sharing allows the user to choose how they want to receive the IT support, thus making the app user-oriented.

Screen sharing

The screen sharining feature comes handy to aid the user in the most optimal way.

Evaluation system

Our evaluation system allows users to rate the help provided by the IT guide and let the guides leave a feedback about thier experience. This rating will help us to improve the service by connecting people with the guides based on their rating score.

Free for all

Our app is completely free of charg and we promise to keep it that way! You got nothing to lose but a lot gain.

Seemless screen sharing = Easy and concrete help!

To aid the user in the most optimal way, we offer a screen sharing alternative, where the users can share their screen with the IT-guide to get help in a quick and easy way.

It's not just awesome! It's secure!
