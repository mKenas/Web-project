Closing the digital gap, one connection at a time.

As technology is evolving faster than ever, the senior generation is struggling to keep up.
Be My It Guide is an app that connects users with technological issues to IT-guides that will aid them in solving them.

×

The world as a whole is becoming more tech oriented every day

The world as we know it is constantly changing and developing, one of the sectors with the fastest development is the tech sector. Technology that once was groundbreaking has now become obsolete. That group is the senior generation. Nowadays everything happens over the internet, this includes banking and digital mailbox systems.
We've come to the point where you must have an online presence in order to manage your life. Our vision is to close the digital gap, therefore making sure that everyone can reap the benefits of technological advancement. Be my IT-Guide introduces video calls as an alternative way to get help with your IT related problems. By helping you solve your technological problems, one connection at a time, we make sure that you also get accustomed to the digital age.

A collaboratory survey done by Telenor and the pension fund shows that half of the Norwegian population, from the ages of 34-50, are struggling to keep up with technological advancements in society. The percentage only increases with age and many fear a digital divide. A different survey done by the Telenor shows that 19 percent of the older generation feel like outsiders because they don’t use net-based banking systems and other official services, the same amount even said to have a case of digital anxiety. The older generation thinks that the youngsters spend too much online, and fear that this may create a digital divide between the generations. (Read more here)

How it works

Be my IT-guide is dedicated to helping solve day-to-day tech problems. We connect the helpers to those that need their service. Users can talk directly to their guides through screen-sharing and chat/video calls making IT problem solving quick and easy.

Features

Quick and easy registration!

The registration process is easier than ever! After picking if you need help or want to help, all you have to is to type in your email and verify it. You can also register using Facebook or Google+.

Get help in your language!

The app lets users select their choice of language to ensure that language barriers do not become a factor in the problem-solving.

Chat/video

Having both chat, and video sharing allows the user to choose how they want to receive the IT support, making the app user-friendly.

Screen sharing

The screen sharing feature is a handy way to aid the user solve their IT-related problems. Screen sharing minimizes potential misunderstandings.

24/7 support

Thanks to our global community we are available to help 24/7. Be my IT guide will be at your service even when your loved ones are not there to help you.

Evaluation system

Our evaluation system allows users to rate the help provided by the IT guide and let the guides leave a feedback about their experience. This rating will help us to improve the service by connecting people with the guides based on their rating score.

Free for all

Our app is completely free to use, and we promise to keep it that way! This ensures that everyone that needs help receives it, regardless of their economic status.

Trophy section

Helping others has never been this much fun! Earn trophies by helping others and show off to your friends and colleagues. Earn special trophies by helping several days in a row. Who knows, this might even impress your future employers.

Ranking system

If collecting trophies isn't enough for you we also have a ranking system! Compete with others both on the local and global stage. Show your knowledge and prove your dedication, do you have what it takes to reach the top?

Tips and tricks

We even have a tips section, to help you with everything from everyday essentials to more advanced issues.

Seamless screen sharing = Easy assistance tailored specifically for you!

To aid the user in the most optimal way, we offer a screen sharing alternative, where the users can share their screen with the IT-guide to get help in a quick and easy way.

It's not just awesome! It's secure!

Securing the privacy of users is our top priority. The screen sharing will not give the IT-guides complete access to your device, thanks to our smart video recording feature, it will send a recording of your screen and restricts the guides’ access to the user's personal information. Every user-to-guide interaction will be recorded, to ensure that there will not be any security breaches.
